Love of Christ Urges Us

The Teresa Fardella Palliative and Hospice Centre, the first initiative of Two Hearts Charitable Trust, provides compassionate and professional care to terminally ill patients. While our vision is to offer free, accessible care to all, the real cost of quality medical and nursing services is high.

To sustain operations, we charge a minimal fee that helps cover basic expenses such as staff salaries, medicines, utilities, and meals. However, this only meets a portion of our needs.

To bridge the gap—and to continue serving the financially poor and the abandoned—we humbly seek the support of donors and benefactors.

Our Services

Compassionate, faith-driven care and support services powered by the generosity of donors and the dedication of trained volunteers, rooted in the love of the Two Hearts of Jesus and Mary.

In-Patient Palliative Care

We provide 24/7 medical and nursing care for patients with serious or terminal illnesses. Our team ensures pain management, symptom relief, emotional support, and spiritual accompaniment in a peaceful, home-like setting.

Learn More

Hospice Care

For those nearing the end of life, we offer compassionate hospice services that focus on comfort, dignity, and emotional support for both patients and their families.

Learn More

Day Care Centre for Elderly Women

We operate a day care program for elderly women, offering them a nurturing space for companionship, nourishment, prayer, and productive engagement.

Learn More

Home Care Services

For patients who prefer to stay at home, we offer professional home visits by nurses and caregivers to monitor health, manage symptoms, and provide guidance to families.

Learn More

Women Empowerment

Through our community initiatives, we empower women—especially the elderly and marginalized—by encouraging self-employment, creative expression, and community participation.

Learn More

Volunteer Training and Formation

We believe that compassionate service begins with formation. Our structured volunteer training programs prepare individuals to serve effectively in hospice care, elderly support, and home visits.

Learn More

Teresa Fardella Palliative and Hospice Centre

Our facilities are thoughtfully designed to offer a peaceful, supportive, and homelike environment where healing, comfort, and dignity come together for patients and their families.

Comfortable In-Patient Area

Spacious, clean, and well-ventilated area with beds, side tables, and seating for visitors.

24/7 Medical and Nursing Care

Our trained team of doctors, nurses, and caregivers are available round the clock to manage symptoms, provide medications, and respond to emergencies with compassion and professionalism.

Day Care Facilities

A welcoming and engaging environment for elderly to spend the day in safety and companionship. Includes workspace for pickle and snack preparation, rest areas, and recreational activities.

Prayer and Meditation Room

A serene chapel space for reflection, prayer, and spiritual nourishment
